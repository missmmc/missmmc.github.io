Oyster Stew

Ingredients

1 pint oysters

4 tablesppons butter

1 quart milk

salt & pepper to taste

Shuck oysters, make sure all bits of shell are removed.

Place oysters in a saucepan with the butter and cook gently unitl the edges of the oysters begin to curl.

Scald milk.

Combine milk and oysters, add salt and pepper to taste.

Serves 6.
